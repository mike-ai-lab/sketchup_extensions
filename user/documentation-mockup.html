Overview

AutoNestCut is an industrial-grade SketchUp extension designed for precision material optimization, waste reduction, and professional workshop documentation.

What's New in v2.0

The latest version focuses on workshop automation and seamless hand-off to CNC production workflows.

Smart Matching

Intelligent fuzzy matching suggests compatible materials from your existing local database.

3D Assembly

Explore assemblies in a native 3D viewer with real-time component highlighting.

QR Labeling

Automated label sheets with QR codes for precise part tracking in the workshop.

CNC Ready

Flatten 3D models into clean vector SVG files ready for laser or router cutting.

System Requirements

Minimum Version: SketchUp 2020 Pro or higher is required. A stable internet connection is needed for initial material sync and license verification.

Core Capabilities

Designed to scale from small custom shops to high-volume fabrication facilities.

Automated Detection

Stop manually sorting components. Our algorithm scans your model to find sheet-good parts based on thickness and grain orientation tags automatically.

Export Ecosystem

  • Smart PDF: Annotated cutting diagrams with grain direction indicators.
  • Interactive HTML: Shareable mobile-friendly reports for the workshop floor.
  • SVG Vectors: Zero-cleanup exports for CNC software like VCarve or LightBurn.
